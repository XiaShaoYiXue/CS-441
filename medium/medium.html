Brushstroke of Silence

By Xiaonan Li and Molly Han

Through silver prints and lithographs they reign,
On penciled paper, their visions remain.
Five sacred mediums men claim as throne,
While women's hands work shadows, oft unknown.
For gelatin silver prints, the most common medium, male artists' works outnumber female artists' by nearly 6 to 1.
