Brushstroke of Silence

By Xiaonan Li and Molly Han

In every wing where art resides,
Male names dominate on all sides.
Each department wall with frames of gold,
Yet women's names stay dim and cold.
Across all MoMA departments, male artists are represented 5 times more than female artists, with the greatest disparity in Architecture & Design.
